Suggested brief structure

  • Project type – PK/PD analytics, CDISC, HEOR/ICER, automation, or a combination.
  • Study / program context – Phase, indication, key endpoints.
  • Key deliverables – e.g., PK tables, ADaM datasets, TLFs, ICER model, dashboards.
  • Data availability – Current format, CDISC status, data standards.
  • Timelines – Regulatory or internal submission dates.
  • Preferred tools – R, Python, Excel/VBA, Shiny, etc.
  • Constraints – Internal templates, governance, or technology limits.
