How We Work

A streamlined, transparent process designed for pharmacology, biometrics, HEOR, and clinical operations teams.

  1. Discovery – Clarify study design, deliverables (PK, TLFs, ICER, automation), and timelines.
  2. Data Ingestion & QC – Ingest PK, clinical, or cost data into a reproducible R/Python pipeline with structured quality checks.
  3. Analytics & Programming – Build PK outputs, ADaM datasets, TLFs, ICER models, or automated reports using validated scripts.
  4. Review & Refinement – Iterate with your team to align tables, figures, and models with internal standards and regulatory expectations.
  5. Optional Internal Tooling – Package workflows as Shiny apps, APIs, or automation scripts for reuse across future studies.
